BAKING

I started baking about a year ago or so. Back in my hometown my sisters and I would bake sweets occasionally. Most of my sisters are sweet tooths and this is something that we have in common. I personally mostly bake three things and I've tried quite a few recipes and found one that I preferred the best.

COOKIES

Cookies are one of the most basic things you can bake, but because it is simple you can have a lot of room to experiment and try ou many different flavour combinations. Over the course of year I have tried almost 7 different cookie recipes and when I found the best one, I made a few changes of my own that aren't in the recipe to make it more my own.

CUPCAKES

I've been wanting to try baking cakes and cupcakes for a long time, but have been wary of investing in trays and such because of limited storage spaces. When I moved in with my friends, I was excited to learn that they have cupcake trays and loaf tins.

With baking, there are many trials and errors to be made. But when the recipe that I have tried for the first time came out really good, I was a little surprised. My favorite sweet thing ever is chocolate anything, so it is natural for me to bake chocolate cupcakes.

BROWNIES

Brownies for me are the most indulgent treat you could have. I started baking brownies back in the Philippines and then forgot about them when I moved to Sydney. After getting back into baking I remembered about the recipe me and my sisters use for brownies. Back then we only made the plain brownies because everything was quite expensive. We would sometimes add a chocolate ganache topping if it were a special occasion or something.

When I moved here, I tried to make it a little bit more interesting because ingredients are a little bit more affordable. By adding different toppings and folding bits of things inside the batter can give the brownie a little bit of texture and a hint of a different flavor as well.
